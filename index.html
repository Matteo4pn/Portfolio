From curiosity to code

From a cook, barista, dance instructor to programmer

I have always been interested in fields in which I could creatively express myself. As a chef in dishes on customers' plates, as a barista taking part in latte art championships, up to dancer and instructor of contemporary dance. I have taught many dance classes, danced in teams at Polish and world championships, and fulfilled my dream: performing in a major production at the Grand Theater of the National Opera in Warsaw.

In the meantime, computer science was part of my life. In every free moment I read about new technologies, listened to podcasts and watched movies centered around this field. I began to play chess professionally and achieved Category IV in classical chess. I started Python CS50 so that I could code for fun. The appearance of text and even images from the code I wrote was like magic to me. I fell down this rabbit hole and currently a day without coding is a lost day for me!
